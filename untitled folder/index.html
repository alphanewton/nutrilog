Features

Everything you need in a health app.

Food and Nutrient Database

NutriLog can offer a comprehensive database of foods and their nutrient profiles, sourced from the Nutritionix API. This can help users track their macro and micronutrient intake and make informed decisions about what they eat.

Analytics and Insights

NutriLog can provide users with detailed analytics and insights on their nutrition habits, such as calorie intake, macro and micronutrient balance, and progress towards their goals. This can help users identify areas for improvement and make adjustments to their diet and exercise regimen accordingly.

Login and Tracking

NutriLog can allow users to create an account and login to track their calorie intake and exercise routine. Users can store their daily calorie intake and calories burned through exercise, making it easy to monitor progress towards their goals.
