Features

Everything you need in a health app.

Food and Nutrient Database

NutriLog can offer a comprehensive database of foods and their nutrient profiles, sourced from the Nutritionix API. This can help users track their macro and micronutrient intake and make informed decisions about what they eat.

Analytics and Insights

NutriLog can provide users with detailed analytics and insights on their nutrition habits, such as calorie intake, macro and micronutrient balance, and progress towards their goals. This can help users identify areas for improvement and make adjustments to their diet and exercise regimen accordingly.

Login and Tracking

NutriLog can allow users to create an account and login to track their calorie intake and exercise routine. Users can store their daily calorie intake and calories burned through exercise, making it easy to monitor progress towards their goals.

Calorie Calcultor

Easy and useful.

Calculate your BMI!

Calculating your Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple and effective way to determine if your weight is within a healthy range.BMI is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared, and the result falls into different categories that indicate if you are underweight, normal weight, overweight or obese.

Calculate Calories Burned!

Nutrilog using NLP, provides a comprehensive database of exercises and their associated calorie burn rates, making it easy to calculate the number of calories burned during different types of physical activity.By integrating the Nutritionix API into a fitness app or website, users can accurately track their calorie burn and adjust their exercise routine to meet their fitness goals.

Calculate Calories Consumed!

Nutrilog offers a vast database of foods and their nutritional information, allowing users to accurately calculate the number of calories consumed in their meals and snacks. By integrating the Nutritionix API into a nutrition app or website, users can easily track their calorie intake and make informed decisions about their diet to achieve their health and fitness goals.
